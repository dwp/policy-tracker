Design Sprint:
Policy Tracker

A quick run through of how we did this design sprint and what the results were.

Day one

This day was about agreeing the problem, understanding the lay of the land and deciding what part of the whole thing we wanted to tackle.

  • Introductions
  • Why are we here?
  • How are things now?
  • How do we want them to be?
  • Sketching exercise

Day two

This day was about starting to delve into the detail. Gathering all the information and content we needed to start to create our prototype.

  • Information gather
  • Page content
  • Paper prototypes
  • Do we have everything we need?

Day three

This day was all about building the prototype, testing our assumptions and preparing to showcase it in our Show ‘n’ Tell.

  • HTML prototype
  • Testing
  • Planning
  • Show ‘n’ Tell
